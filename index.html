WHY IT MATTERS

Why We're Different

Every major resale platform hands you a prepaid label when someone buys from you. That label has to be used. It is tied to the sale, it ships from you, and you cannot hand it off to anyone else. That is by design.

And every single sale still means you physically pack it, label it, and drop it off. That's not a business. That's a part-time job with no ceiling.

The problem is not the platforms. It is the model. When you are the one shipping, you are the bottleneck. There is no way around it unless you change the model entirely.

Vault Pro is the fulfillment layer the platforms don't offer. Your sales go up. Your workload doesn't. That's how you actually scale.
